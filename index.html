What This Tool Does

When you copy text from Word documents, PDFs, AI chatbots, emails, or websites, it often carries invisible formatting baggage — extra spaces, non-breaking characters, smart quotes, long dashes, and hidden Unicode characters that can break your layouts, confuse voice systems, or just look wrong.

This free tool cleans all of that up instantly in your browser. No upload required, no account needed. Your text never leaves your device.

  • Clean AI-generated text before pasting into email
