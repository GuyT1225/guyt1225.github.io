    What PasteLint Does

    When you copy text from Word, PDFs, AI chatbots, emails, or websites it often carries invisible baggage — extra spaces, non-breaking characters, smart quotes, long dashes, and hidden Unicode that breaks layouts, confuses voice systems, or just looks wrong. PasteLint detects exactly what it found, cleans it all up, and shows you precisely what changed — entirely in your browser.

    Need the same fix every time? Save custom find & replace rules that persist between visits. Working with lists or spreadsheet data? Switch to Line-by-Line mode for row-by-row cleaning.

    Common Use Cases

    • Fix copy paste formatting from Word or PDFs
    • Clean AI-generated text before pasting into email or CMS
    • Rewrite AI-sounding text into clearer, more natural writing
    • Remove hidden characters from copied text
    • Prepare text for IVR, Amazon Polly, or text-to-speech systems
    • Lint and sanitize content before publishing to a website
    • Bulk-clean rows of data copied from a spreadsheet
