What Rodan Does

Rodan helps clean up writing that feels too generic, too polished, or too obviously generated by an AI tool. It looks for common AI tells, removes filler, simplifies inflated wording, and keeps the original meaning intact.

It is useful for emails, short posts, product copy, internal notes, public updates, IVR scripts, and drafts that need to sound more natural before publishing.
